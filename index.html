The surge in electric vehicles, renewables, and consumer electronics has escalated the need for advanced Li-ion batteries. Yet, traditional R&D often falls short, requiring extensive time and funding, and yielding inadequate solutions. Enter Recell: pioneering breakthroughs in battery recycling to meet the demands of sustainable energy solutions.

Introducing our groundbreaking innovation, ATLAS: the world's premier rapid, end-to-end automated materials discovery solution for batteries. By seamlessly integrating machine learning, proprietary technology, and rigorous testing, we've revolutionized the development process. Compared to traditional methods, our approach is 37 times faster and 16 times more cost-effective, setting a new standard in battery material discovery

Through strategic alliances with established battery manufacturers, Eonix facilitates the swift scaling of bespoke chemistry Li-ion batteries. This enables end-users in automotive, grid, defense, and electronics sectors to access tailored solutions in familiar formats, expediting adoption and integration.
